# Sample Datasets for CKAN Upload

This folder contains upload-ready sample resources for each seeded Vigan City agency.

Suggested CKAN dataset mapping:

| Agency | Suggested Dataset Title | File |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Business Permits & Licensing | Registered Businesses 2025 | `business-permits/registered-businesses-2025.csv` |
| City Budget Office | City Annual Budget 2025 | `city-budget/city-annual-budget-2025.csv` |
| City Environment & Natural Resources | Solid Waste Collection Routes | `cenro/solid-waste-collection-routes.csv` |
| City Health Office | Public Health Facilities | `city-health/public-health-facilities.csv` |
| City Planning & Development | Barangay Boundaries (GeoJSON) | `city-planning/barangay-boundaries.geojson` |
| Disaster Risk Reduction & Mgmt | Flood Hazard Priority Areas | `drrmo/flood-hazard-priority-areas.geojson` |
| Vigan City Tourism Office | Vigan Heritage Sites Directory | `tourism-office/vigan-heritage-sites.csv` |
| Vigan City Tourism Office | Monthly Tourist Arrivals 2025 | `tourism-office/monthly-tourist-arrivals-2025.csv` |

Notes:

- CSV files are suitable for CKAN preview and DataStore import.
- GeoJSON files are suitable for map-oriented sample resources.
- All files use fictional but plausible sample values for demo and testing purposes.
